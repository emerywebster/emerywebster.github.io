Responding to the Pandemic

Problem – With many gyms closing in March & April of 2020, our customers needed a quick way to adapt to their clients’ limited access to equipment.

Solution – During a team investment day, I partnered with a front end engineer to explore how coaches might more easily build at-home workouts for clients. We introduced a bodyweight tag to our exercise library and introduced new UI to easily find them while building workouts.

Outcome – While the solution below was never fully implemented, we learned that a bodyweight exercise tag provided a quick and inexpensive way to address the problem and proactively support our customers.
